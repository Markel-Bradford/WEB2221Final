Major Tournament Series

Genesis X

February 16th - 18th

Prize Pool: $23,160

Genesis X, anually hosted in San Jose, CA, marked the 10th iteration in one of the longest running major tournament series in Super Smash Bros. Melee history. With over 1250 entrants and thousands of spectators, Genesis continues to be one of the heaviest hitting majors that the community has to offer. Professional player, Cody Schwab took the crown and the prize money this year in exciting fashion, winning every set he played. Genesis will return again for what will be another exciting chapter in the 1st quarter of 2025.

Low Tide City 2024

May 11th - 12th

Prize Pool: TBD

Low Tide City, also known as the Smash Resort, is an event that combines competition and fun at the largest indoor water park in North America. The tournament is hosted at the Kalahari Resort where players can swim, eat, drink, and play to catch a breather inbetween their tournament bouts. It is definitely an event to look forward to for players and fans alike. Unfortunately, Low Tide City 2024 will be the final iteration in this unique series, so you know the community won't miss out on it's closing chapter.

Get On My Level (GOML)

May 17th - 19th

Prize Pool: TBD

Get On My Level, referred to as GOML, is Canada's premier tournament event and is touted as the Tournament of Upsets. For its 10 anniversary event, GOML will be hosted at the Downtown Toronto Hotel and will be a 24 hour accesible venue event. Legends have seen their rise and fall at this event, year in and year out, making for an exciting narrative following each iterations conclusion. You never know what to expect, but as the title of the event depicts, you must be ready to compete and Get On My Level, or fall like the rest!

Tipped Off 15: Conntected

June 15th - 16th

Prize Pool: TBD

Tipped Off 15: Connected is a grassroots series that has whethered the storm of time and become one of the most beloved majors the community has to offer. Hosted in Marietta, GA, the 15th installment set to take place this June promises to be another great tournment for competitors and fans alike. The event will provide a 24 hour venue with several gameplay setups for practice and fun throughout the tournament duration. There is no doubt that this will be must see Twitch.tv!
